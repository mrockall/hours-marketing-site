Hours
Never miss a shift again

Managing work shifts has never been easier

Hours allows a manager to easily create weekly shifts for all employees.
With the click of a button your weekly roster can be sent to all employees.
Once published or changed, on instant notification will be sent to all employees letting them see their hours for that week.

Struggling to find a shift swap becomes a thing of the past

With the tap of a button you can notify your employer that a certain shift doesn’t suit you
A notification will instantly be sent directly to your employer informing them of the problem
Employers can edit shifts from right within the app so that everyone is happy.

The benefits leave little to be desired

Helpful suggestions prompt you to fill out upcoming rosters from scratch or using lasts weeks as a template
Rosters can be imported from Excel if that how you’re used to doing things
Employers and employees alike can integrate the shifts right into their personal calendars or export to PDF
